# BigCommerce Catalyst (and Stencil)

BigCommerce's two storefront stacks.

- **Stencil** — the legacy server-rendered theme framework (handlebars-flavored). Still widely deployed.
- **Catalyst** — the modern Next.js-based reference storefront BigCommerce ships, similar in role to Shopify's [[hydrogen]].

## Why they show up together in Nikita's CV

20+ live BigCommerce client stores spanning both stacks (per [[2026-05-05-02-cv-merge-speroteck]]).

- 30+ live storefronts shipped in 5 years

**Skills** — modernized to current stack: BigCommerce Stencil/Catalyst, NestJS, gRPC, RabbitMQ, AWS Lambda / Fly.io / Docker, OAuth 2.0, ADA. Dropped dated Salesforce/SFCC/Apex.

**Education** — added NODE-JS-PRO course + GoIT bootcamp.

**Career timeline** — split into two clean dated lines: [[speroteck]] (2022–present) and [[ipc2u]] (2016–2022).
